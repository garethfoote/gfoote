Designer working in the public sector and lecturer in design and creative computing. 

I'm currently a Lead Designer at the [London Borough of Camden](https://www.camden.gov.uk/). Formally, Senior Service and Interaction Designer at [The National Archives](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/). Alongside this, I am a part-time Associate Lecturer at the [Creative Computing Institute](https://www.arts.ac.uk/creative-computing-institute), and formerly Course Leader for [BA (Hons) User Experience Design](https://www.arts.ac.uk/subjects/animation-interactive-film-and-sound/undergraduate/ba-hons-user-experience-design-lcc) at University of the Arts London.
